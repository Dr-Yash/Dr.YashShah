About Me

Dr. Yash Maulik Shah is Training and Placement Officer with over 05 years of experience in the education sector. He is passionate about helping students to achieve their career goals and has a proven track record of success.


Yash Maulik Shah is responsible for managing the entire placement process for Sankalchand Patel University. This includes sourcing and screening potential employers, preparing students for interviews, and negotiating salaries. He also provides students with career counseling and guidance.


In his previous role, Dr. Yash Maulik Shah was the Training Officer at Rashtriya Raksha University, Ministry of Home Affairs, and Government of India. During his time there, he was a part of training programs for National security personals. He is a Master's in Business Administration for Education Management and a Doctorate Degree in Mass communication and Journalism. He has contributed research for National Security over a span of Three years now.


Dr. Yash Maulik Shah is a highly motivated and results-oriented individual. He is also a team player and has a strong work ethic. He is confident that he can make a significant contribution in the Educational sector.
